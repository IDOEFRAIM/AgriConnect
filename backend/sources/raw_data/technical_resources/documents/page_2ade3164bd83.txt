Cahiers Agricultures
Cahiers Agricultures
is a - mainly - French language scientific journal on world farming systems, how they are changing and their role in society. It is aimed at all those – researchers, field workers, teachers – who are interested in a holistic reflection on the agricultural world.
Cahiers Agricultures
is a “Diamond” Open access journal; all papers are freely available without subscription or publication fees for authors as it is sponsored by
CIRAD
and
IRD
. The journal celebrated
30 years of publication in 2022
.
